---
title: "DigiComLab End-of-Year Event"
date: 2026-06-22
---


Last week, DigiComLab hosted a successful end-of-year event bringing together colleagues and Research Master’s students. During the event, six student grant recipients and four faculty seed grant recipients presented their ongoing research.

The presentations showcased a wide range of topics, methods, and approaches, including interactions with social robots, news exposure on social media, and several studies on interactions with large language models. The event offered a valuable opportunity to exchange ideas, learn about ongoing projects, and connect across the DigiComLab community.

We thank everyone who joined us and contributed to this engaging afternoon. We look forward to continuing these conversations in the next academic year.
